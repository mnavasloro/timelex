What is TimeLex?

It is a suite of services that target the processing of the temporal information dimension of legal texts. It includes different contributions to the temporal information extraction task in the legal domain, outlined below:

  1. LawORDate: a tool that finds and replaces misleading legal references in legal texts to enhance the temporal tagging task.
  2. Añotador: temporal tagger specially tuned to overcome and cover specifities in the temporal dimension of legal texts.
  3. WhenTheFact: a tool that finds relevant events in decisions form the European Court of Human Rights, along with information such as the subject, when they happen and if their type in a dedicated classification. It both highlights these events and their arguments and buids a timeline based on these findings.

For additional information of each of the tools, please select it in the menu in the left side of the webpage.
