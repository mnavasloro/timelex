What is it?

WhenTheFact is a tool that extracts relevant events from judgments from European Courts, namely from the European Court of Human Rights and the European Court of Justice. Below is a screenshot of the demo.

How it works?

The scheme below illustrates how WhenTheFact works.

Once the url or the ID of judgment to process is submitted, the system retrieves it from the source and analyzes its structure, detecting the relevant sections in it. Then, the sentences from these parts of the documents are processed in order to find the dates in it. Afterwards, the relevant events are searched and linkked to their happening dates, if any. Finally, they are marked up in the text, and a timeline with the most relevant events that the user can use to navigate through the document is created.
